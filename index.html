What We Do

Comprehensive Federal Programs Support

We provide end-to-end consulting services that allow school leaders to focus on students — while we handle the compliance, documentation, and reporting demands of federal programs.

📋

Grant Management & Compliance

Monitoring expenditures, maintaining documentation, and ensuring full compliance with ESEA/ESSA requirements. We coordinate with the Georgia Department of Education on audits, monitoring visits, and grant-related inquiries — so nothing falls through the cracks.

🏫

Title I & Federal Programs Administration

Full administration of Title I, Part A programs including Consolidated Application (ConApp) preparation, Comprehensive Needs Assessments, School Improvement Plans, and fiscal documentation. We ensure your programs are properly documented, allowable, and aligned with federal statute.

🎓

Professional Development & Training

Customized professional development sessions for instructional and administrative staff on federal program requirements, allowable activities, and data-driven decision making. We also develop onboarding materials for staff new to federal programs administration.

About

Meet the Consultant

Madison Endert

Founder & Principal Consultant

8+ Years of Experience

Madison Endert founded Endert Educational Consulting, LLC to bring the kind of on-the-ground expertise that only comes from having done the work — as a Federal Programs Director inside a charter school, she navigated every layer of ESSA compliance, Title I administration, and GaDOE reporting firsthand.

With over eight years of experience in federal education programs, Madison understands the pressure school leaders face when grant deadlines, audit requests, and programmatic requirements arrive all at once. Her practice is built on translating complex federal requirements into clear, actionable guidance that schools can actually implement.

Grounded in federal law, GaDOE regulations, and 2 C.F.R. Part 200 cost principles, Madison partners with charter schools across Georgia to ensure their programs are compliant, well-documented, and squarely focused on student outcomes.
